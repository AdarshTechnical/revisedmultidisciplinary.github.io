Condition of Farmers & Artisans

The Farmers and artisans were living a nice life. But when the British rule took over the Diwani then also it saw themselves as a traders.And they did not wanted any duties and their trade. Farmers and artisan were forced to sell their goods to the company at lower rates. This lead to a great famine in which one third of the population of the Bengal was wiped out.Farmers were forced to give their land and they could not pay the high taxes demanded by Britishers.


The farmer were ordered to grow a certain crop demanded by the Britishers. Condition of farmers and artisans was dismal during the colonial period.They also kept dying during this period because of famine and other causes. This eventually lead to many rebels and the great and huge rebel of 1857.
