Six metals and their uses in Prehistoric period

Prehistoric men is known to have used six metals.Those were:-


Gold- Gold is very soft and was therefore predominantly used for decoration and bullion for trade.


Silver-Silver was easy to collect and relatively easy to shape therefore it was used for making jewellery.


Copper-Copper had more qualities and could be hardened by hammering and therefore used to make tools.


Tin- Tin was a major material used for making bronze.


Lead-Lead is soft,easily worked and it realised that it was perfect for making into vessels to transport liquids.


Iron- Iron was such an important material,used for all sort of applications.
